Frequently Asked Questions

Here, you can find frequently asked questions that may be on your mind.

  • Can you get banned for using Juicy Swapper?

    No. There is currently less than a 0.1% chance of being banned whilst using Juicy Swapper, and this is very unlikely, however please use at your own risk. Juicy Swapper is not held responsible for any bans.

  • Is Juicy Swapper a virus?

    No. Juicy Swapper is completely free of malware. You will need to disable any anti-viruses because Juicy Swapper is detected as a 'false positive' which means your computer thinks it's a virus, however it's not.

  • Why do I get kicked from matches?

    This is most likely because you are swapping too many items at a time, which causes Fortnite to think that your game files are 'corrupt' because too many lines of code have been changed. The maximum item swap is 4.

  • What benefits come with nitro boosting/donating?

    You unlock plenty of benefits, which range from a special role positioned above all others to rep your support, all the way to priority support to make sure you have the best experience.
